Maria Yurlova

makeup artist

Brief History - I am a makeup artist from Moscow, Russia. Traveling, experiencing new things in life, learning from different cultures and international people are some of the most valuable aspects for me. Currently, I am studying Chemical Engineering at the University of Houston while doing something that I love - applying makeup.

I have always had passion for beauty and esthetics. To me, makeup is art. I love making women of all ages feel beautiful, confident, and empowered. Sometimes, all it takes, is a touch of makeup.

My favorite look: contoured face, brown smokey eye, and natural lip. This makeup may compliment any face type and be performed for both daytime and evening wear. I am passionate for homecoming, prom, and wedding looks. I also love working with mature skin. It is possible to look 10 years younger just by using the right makeup techniques! Whether you are feeling daring or, rather, prefer something neutral, we can work it out for you.
